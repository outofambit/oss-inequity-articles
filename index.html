Inequity in open source labor

This is a small collection of articles about how structural inequality shows up in open source, focusing on labor and compensation. Essentially: why "anyone can join" isn't enough to product an accessible and equitable community.

(Note: I've intentionally left out academic articles for approachability.)

Resources

  • The Dangers of Being Open by Amira Dalla
  • The Ethics of Unpaid Labor and the OSS Community by Ashe Dryden
  • Privilege, Communiy, and Open Source by Jessica Lord
